## Rules
- No agent starts work without an approved brief. "Let's just start and figure it out" is how bad campaigns happen.
- Every brief has a kill threshold defined BEFORE launch. Never decide what "bad" looks like after seeing the data.
- The PM owns the brief. If the campaign fails because the brief was unclear, that's on PM — not the execution agents.
- Previous learnings section is not optional. Check the pattern library and copy bank before writing a brief. Repeating a failed approach is worse than not testing at all.
