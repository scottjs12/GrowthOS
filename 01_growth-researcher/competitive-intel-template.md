## Monitoring Cadence

| Source | Frequency | What to Watch |
|---|---|---|
| Product changelog / blog | Monthly | New features, positioning shifts |
| Meta Ad Library | Monthly | Creative angles, spend signals |
| G2 / Capterra / App Store reviews | Monthly | User complaints = our opportunity |
| LinkedIn job postings | Quarterly | Hiring signals = strategic direction |
| Funding announcements | As they happen | Capital = capability (eventually) |
| Social media | Weekly (passive) | Messaging shifts, viral moments |

## Rules

- Be honest about competitor strengths. "They suck" is not intelligence — it's cope.
- Update quarterly minimum. Stale competitor intel is worse than no intel.
- Track what they DO, not what they SAY. Press releases are marketing. Product changes and hiring are strategy.
- Every competitor profile should answer: "If a prospect is comparing us, what do we say?" If you can't answer that, the profile is incomplete.
- Never track more than 5 competitors actively. Focus on the ones your customers actually compare you to.
