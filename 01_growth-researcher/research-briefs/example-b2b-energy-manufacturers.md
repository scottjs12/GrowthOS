──────────────
Date:       2026-03-23
For:        Content Writer, Paid, CRO, Designer
Source:     ICP research synthesis — manufacturing operations + sustainability personas
Finding:    Manufacturers evaluate energy solutions through ROI specificity and peer
            proof — not features or technology claims. The #1 objection is vendor
            distrust ("promised 20%, delivered 4%"), not price.
Evidence:
  1. Verbatim: "Show me the ROI or don't bother scheduling a call" (manufacturing forum)
  2. Verbatim: "Every vendor says 'AI-powered' but can't explain what the AI actually does"
  3. Pattern: Operations VPs trust peer referrals and case studies from same vertical
     over any marketing claim. They evaluate through reference calls, not demos.
Implication:
  - Content Writer: Lead with guaranteed/quantified outcomes, not product features.
    Avoid "AI-powered" and "smart energy" language entirely.
  - Paid: Case study creative will outperform product demo creative for this ICP.
    Test proof angle before demo angle.
  - CRO: Guarantee badge + specific timeline ("48-hour audit") will neutralize
    trust objection more effectively than testimonials alone.
  - Designer: Dashboard screenshots > stock factory imagery. Show the product working.
Suggested action: Build all LP and ad creative around proof + specificity angles first.
                  Test pain angle second. Deprioritize feature/demo angles until trust
                  is established through proof-led campaigns.
Urgency: Routine
