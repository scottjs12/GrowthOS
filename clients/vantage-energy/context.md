## Content Writer Context

**Brand voice:**
- Professional but not corporate. Confident, direct, grounded in results. Vantage talks like the trusted advisor who's been doing this for 25 years — no need to oversell, the numbers speak for themselves.
- **Tone:** Confident, straightforward, trustworthy, calm authority
- **Never sound like:** Aggressive sales, hype, overpromising, startup jargon, "disruptive"

**Voice references:** The confidence of a seasoned consultant. Think accounting firm meets plain-spoken advisor.

**Signature themes:**
- "Stop overpaying for utilities"
- "We find the errors. You keep the savings."
- "No upfront cost. No obligation. Just answers."

**Good examples:** (from current site)
- "We audit your bills, recover overcharges, and fix it going forward, without adding work to your team."
- "100% contingency — if we don't find savings, you pay nothing."
- "Four Steps. Zero Hassle."

**Never write:**
- "Revolutionary energy platform" or any tech-startup language
- "Leverage our cutting-edge analytics" — Vantage is people-driven, not a SaaS
- Anything that implies risk or complexity for the client

**Primary ICP:** CFOs and facility managers at education, healthcare, and nonprofit organizations who are overpaying on utilities but don't have the time or expertise to audit their own bills.

**Top converting angles:**
- Pain-led: "90% of utility bills contain errors. Yours probably does too."
- Identity-led: "Built for organizations that can't afford waste."
- Proof-led: "$10M+ recovered. 75% of clients get refunds. Average recovery: $127K."
