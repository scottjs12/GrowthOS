# Messaging Matrix

> Owner: Content Writer | Source of truth for positioning and message-to-audience mapping
> Updated when Researcher surfaces new ICP insights or Strategist calls a positioning shift.

---

## Format

```
SEGMENT:         [ICP segment name]
CORE TENSION:    [The thing that keeps them up at night — verbatim language if possible]
PRIMARY MESSAGE: [One sentence that speaks directly to the tension]
PROOF POINT:     [Specific, credible evidence]
CTA:             [The single next action]
CHANNEL FIT:     [Which channels this message works best on]
```

---

## Segments

> **Example entries below** — generated as a GrowthOS demo for a fictional B2B energy company.

### Operations VP — Mid-Market Manufacturers

**Segment:** Operations VP — Mid-Market Manufacturers
**Core tension:** "Energy is eating our margins and I can't see where it's going"
**Primary message:** Cut energy waste across your plants — without touching production
**Proof point:** "Manufacturers using [Product] reduce energy spend 18-22% in Year 1"
**CTA:** Get your free plant energy audit
**Channel fit:** LinkedIn ads, Google Search, cold email

### Sustainability/EHS Director

**Segment:** Sustainability/EHS Director
**Core tension:** "I have a reduction target and no reliable data to hit it"
**Primary message:** Track, measure, and prove your energy reductions — plant by plant
**Proof point:** "Audit-ready Scope 1+2 reporting across all facilities in one view"
**CTA:** See your emissions baseline in 5 minutes
**Channel fit:** LinkedIn organic, content/SEO, industry events
