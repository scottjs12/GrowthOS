# Social Content Playbook

> Framework for social media content strategy across platforms. Adapt content pillars, posting cadence, and templates to each client's product and ICP.

## Content Pillars

Every social strategy needs 4-6 repeating content pillars. Pillars create consistency and make batching possible.

**Default pillar framework (customize per client):**

| Pillar | Purpose | Example Format | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Product in Action** | Show the core value moment | Short video, screen recording, demo clip | 3-5x/week |
| **Discovery / Surprise** | Create dopamine hits, shareability | Reveal videos, unexpected results, hidden features | 2-3x/week |
| **Social Proof** | Build trust through peer validation | Testimonials, user stories, milestone celebrations | 2-3x/week |
| **Education** | Establish authority, help beginners | How-to videos, tips, explainers, myth-busting | 1-2x/week |
| **Market / Industry** | Stay relevant, ride trending topics | Reactive posts, news commentary, trend takes | 1-2x/week |

**Rule:** Every post must fit a pillar. If it doesn't fit, either create a new pillar or don't post it. Random content dilutes brand.

## Platform Strategy Matrix

Adapt pillar mix, tone, and frequency per platform. Not every platform needs every pillar.

| Platform | Frequency | Top Pillars | Tone | Format Focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **TikTok** | 5-7x/week | Product in Action (40%), Discovery (25%), Education (20%) | Casual, unpolished, reactive | Short video (15-60s) |
| **Instagram** | 4-5x/week | Product in Action (30%), Social Proof (25%), Discovery (20%) | Clean, polished, aspirational | Reels, carousels, Stories |
| **Twitter/X** | 2-3x/day | Market/Industry (40%), Product in Action (25%), Engagement (20%) | Sharp, fast, opinionated | Text, threads, quote tweets |
| **LinkedIn** | 3-4x/week | Education (35%), Social Proof (30%), Market (20%) | Professional, insight-driven | Text posts, carousels, articles |
| **Facebook** | 3-4x/week | Discovery (30%), Product in Action (30%), Education (20%) | Community-oriented, conversational | Video, images, group posts |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 3-5x/week | Product in Action (35%), Discovery (25%), Education (20%) | Slightly polished, informative | Short video (15-60s) |

## Video Content Templates

### Template 1: Product in Action (15-30 seconds)
```
VIDEO: PRODUCT IN ACTION
─────────────────────────
Hook (0-3s):     [Bold text overlay + motion — grab attention]
Setup (3-8s):    [Show the starting point / problem / "before" state]
Action (8-20s):  [Product doing the thing — the core value moment]
Result (20-25s): [The outcome — show the number, the change, the benefit]
CTA (25-30s):    [Text overlay: "Try it yourself" / "Link in bio" / app store badge]
```

### Template 2: Discovery / Surprise (30-60 seconds)
```
VIDEO: DISCOVERY
────────────────
Hook (0-3s):     [Curiosity trigger — "Look what I found" / "You won't believe"]
Buildup (3-15s): [Show the context — where, what, why this is interesting]
Reveal (15-40s): [The surprise moment — the dopamine hit. Montage if multiple items]
Payoff (40-50s): [Total value, summary, or wow stat]
CTA (50-60s):    [Soft CTA — "Have you tried this?" / "Link in bio"]
```

### Template 3: Education (20-35 seconds)
```
VIDEO: EDUCATION
────────────────
Hook (0-3s):     [Question or bold claim — "Most people get this wrong"]
Problem (3-10s): [Name the misconception or knowledge gap]
Answer (10-25s): [Simple, clear explanation — one concept only]
Proof (25-30s):  [Example, stat, or visual demonstration]
CTA (30-35s):    ["Follow for more" / "Save this" / "Link in bio"]
```

### Template 4: Market / Trending (15-30 seconds)
```
VIDEO: MARKET / TRENDING
────────────────────────
Hook (0-3s):     [The event or news — what just happened]
Context (3-10s): [Why it matters to your audience]
Implication (10-20s): [What this means for them — price change, opportunity, risk]
CTA (20-30s):    ["What do you think?" / engagement prompt]
```

### Template 5: Social Proof (30-45 seconds)
```
VIDEO: SOCIAL PROOF
───────────────────
Hook (0-3s):     [The result — lead with the impressive number or outcome]
Story (3-20s):   [How they got there — the user's journey in brief]
Highlights (20-35s): [Best moments, key milestones, specific achievements]
CTA (35-45s):    ["Start your own [journey]" / "Join [X] others"]
```

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

- Sound like a knowledgeable peer, not a brand. Conversational, not corporate.
- Lead with the value or the hook — not the brand name.
- Use specific numbers: "$487 saved" beats "save money." "10,247 users" beats "thousands."
- Be reactive to real-world events when relevant to your audience.
- No corporate jargon. No excessive emojis. No hashtag spam (max 3-5 per post).
- Reply to comments within 4 hours during business hours.
- Quote-tweet and reshare community/user content — UGC is the highest-trust content you can post.

## Performance Rules

| Signal | Action |
|---|---|
| Post gets 2x normal reach | Boost with $10-20 paid spend |
| Post gets 50%+ saves or shares | Repurpose the format — it's a winner |
| Engagement rate <1% for 2 weeks | Audit content pillars and hooks |
| Follower growth stalls for 30 days | Test new content format or pillar |
| Negative comments spike | Investigate, respond transparently, escalate if needed |

## Content Repurposing Flow

```
Long-form video (YouTube / podcast)
    ↓ clip into
Short-form video (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
    ↓ screenshot key frame
Static post (Instagram feed, LinkedIn)
    ↓ extract quote
Text post (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
    ↓ compile insights
Carousel (Instagram, LinkedIn)
    ↓ email
Newsletter feature (weekly digest)
```

**Rule:** One piece of hero content should produce 5-8 derivative pieces. If you're creating everything from scratch, you're working too hard.
