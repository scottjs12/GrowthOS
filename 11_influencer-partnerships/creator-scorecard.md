# Creator Scorecard

> Quantified vetting before you commit budget. Gut feel gets expensive — score every creator before outreach.

## Scoring Criteria (1-5 each, total /30)

| Criteria | 1 (Poor) | 3 (Acceptable) | 5 (Excellent) | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Audience Fit** | <30% overlap with ICP | 50-70% overlap | >80% overlap with ICP | 2x |
| **Engagement Rate** | <1% (inflated/bot) | 2-4% (healthy) | >5% (highly engaged) | 2x |
| **Content Quality** | Low effort, inconsistent | Decent production, regular cadence | Professional, on-brand, consistent | 1x |
| **Audience Size** | Doesn't match tier needs | Matches campaign tier | Sweet spot for reach goals | 1x |
| **Brand Safety** | Controversial, risky content | Neutral, professional | Values-aligned, brand-safe | 1x |
| **Past Performance** | No data or poor results | Average benchmark results | Proven conversions, repeat partner | 1x |

Weighted total: /40 (Audience Fit and Engagement Rate are 2x weighted)

## Engagement Rate Benchmarks

| Platform | Nano (<10K) | Micro (10-100K) | Mid (100K-500K) | Macro (500K-1M) | Mega (1M+) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 4-8% | 2-5% | 1.5-3% | 1-2% | 0.5-1.5% |
| TikTok | 8-15% | 5-10% | 3-7% | 2-5% | 1-3% |
| YouTube | 5-10% | 3-7% | 2-5% | 1.5-3% | 1-2% |
| Twitter/X | 2-5% | 1-3% | 0.5-2% | 0.3-1% | 0.1-0.5% |

**How to calculate:** (Likes + Comments) / Followers x 100. Use average of last 10 posts, exclude outlier viral posts.

## Score Thresholds

| Score | Action |
|---|---|
| 32-40 | **Pursue immediately** — prioritize outreach |
| 24-31 | **Proceed with caution** — negotiate terms carefully, start with one-off |
| 16-23 | **Deprioritize** — only if no better options in the niche |
| <16 | **Pass** — audience or engagement doesn't justify investment |

## Red Flags (Auto-Disqualify)

- [ ] Follower/following ratio <2:1 (follow-for-follow growth)
- [ ] Engagement drops >50% in last 3 months (bought followers decaying)
- [ ] Comments are generic ("nice!" "love this!") — bot engagement
- [ ] No branded content history (may not know how to integrate products naturally)
- [ ] Audience location mismatch (80% of followers in non-target geography)

## Rules
- Score BEFORE outreach. Never negotiate without a scorecard on file.
- Re-score every 6 months for ongoing partnerships — audiences shift.
- Nano/micro creators often outperform macro on conversion rate. Don't dismiss small audiences.
- Always check 3 months of content history, not just the highlight reel.
- If engagement rate is high but comments are low-quality, investigate before scoring.
