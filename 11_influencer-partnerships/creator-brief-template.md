# Creator Brief Template

> This is what the creator receives. It should be clear enough that they can produce content without a call. If they need a call after reading this, the brief failed.

## Brief

```
CREATOR BRIEF
─────────────
Brand:
Campaign:
Creator:
Platform:         [Instagram | TikTok | YouTube | Twitter]
Content type:     [Feed post | Reel | Story | TikTok | YouTube video | Short]
Deliverables:     [Number of pieces, formats]
Due date:
Review deadline:  [Must submit for review X days before publish date]
```

## Objective
- Campaign goal: [Awareness | Signups | App installs | Purchases]
- What success looks like: [Specific metric — e.g., "Drive signups via link in bio"]
- Target audience: [Who should this content reach — describe the viewer, not demographics]

## Key Messages
- **Must include:**
  1. [Core message/value prop — one sentence]
  2. [Specific feature or benefit to highlight]
  3. [CTA — what should the viewer do?]

- **Must avoid:**
  - [Competitor mentions — never name competitors]
  - [Claims we can't substantiate]
  - [Off-brand language or topics]

## Creative Direction
- **Tone:** [Authentic, educational, entertaining, aspirational — pick 1-2]
- **Format guidance:**
  - Hook: First 3 seconds must stop the scroll. Lead with [outcome / question / surprising fact].
  - Body: Show, don't tell. [Demo the product / share personal experience / show results]
  - CTA: End with clear action. [Link in bio / swipe up / use code]
- **Visual requirements:**
  - [ ] Product must be visible and clearly identifiable
  - [ ] Good lighting and audio quality
  - [ ] Vertical format (9:16) for Stories/Reels/TikTok
  - [ ] No competing brand logos visible

## Talking Points (use naturally, don't read verbatim)
1. [Point 1 — the problem the product solves]
2. [Point 2 — what makes it different]
3. [Point 3 — personal experience or result]

## Compliance & Disclosure
- [ ] FTC disclosure required: #ad or #sponsored must be visible (not buried in hashtags)
- [ ] Paid partnership tag enabled on platform (Instagram, TikTok)
- [ ] No health, financial, or results claims without approval
- [ ] All claims must be the creator's honest experience

## Tracking
- Unique link: [vanity URL or UTM link]
- Promo code: [if applicable]
- Hashtag: [branded hashtag if applicable]

## Usage Rights
- Usage period: [30 days | 60 days | 90 days | perpetual]
- Organic usage: [Yes / No — can brand repost?]
- Paid amplification: [Yes / No — can brand run as ad?]
- Platforms: [Where can brand use the content?]

## Review Process
1. Creator submits draft by [date]
2. Brand reviews within 48h
3. Max 1 round of revisions (unless brief compliance issue)
4. Creator publishes on [date]
5. Creator shares analytics within 7 days of posting

## Rules
- Never script the creator word-for-word. Authenticity drives performance.
- The brief should fit on one page. If it's longer, you're over-directing.
- Creators know their audience better than you. Give direction, not dictation.
- If the creator pushes back on creative direction, listen. They usually have a good reason.
